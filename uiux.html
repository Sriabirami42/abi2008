UI/UX Design – PawMart App

Introduction:

In the UI/UX design session, I explored modern design principles using Figma. As an assignment, I created an online dog essentials store – PawMart – to understand layout composition, spacing, font hierarchy, color schemes, and user flow.

Objectives:

  • Design a mobile-friendly UI for an online dog essentials store.
  • Practice layout, typography, and alignment in Figma.
  • Simulate screen flows like login, explore, and product pages.

Tools & Techniques Used:

  • Figma Components: Buttons, icons, navbars, cards reused across screens.
  • Auto Layout: For spacing and responsive design.
  • Color Palettes: Soft, pet-themed colors for friendly appearance.
  • Plugins: Used Material Icons and Google Fonts for clarity.
  • Prototyping: Simulated navigation flow between screens.

Pages Designed:

  • Signup/Login Page
  • Product Listing Page (Food, Grooming, Toys)
  • Product Detail View
  • Cart & Checkout Screen

Design Preview:

Conclusion:

This project strengthened my understanding of UI structure, hierarchy, and consistency. Designing PawMart helped me analyze layout strategies and component reusability for real-world mobile apps.

In the future, I aim to design my own original apps and collaborate on live UI/UX projects with developers.
